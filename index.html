Summary

I am a teaching assistant and a PhD student from the Department of IT and Management of UMONS supervised by Mickael Randour and Xavier Siebert.

My research focuses on the formal synthesis problem for sochastic systems evolving on continuous-state-spaces. More specifically, I'm interested in techniques based on finite abstraction of the system or techniques mixing formal methods and learning approaches.
